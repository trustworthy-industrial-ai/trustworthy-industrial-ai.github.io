# Research Roadmap

Engineering Trustworthy Industrial AI

Version: 1.0

Status: Living Document

Last Updated: July 2026

---

# Purpose

This roadmap describes the long-term research agenda of the Engineering Trustworthy Industrial AI initiative.

Rather than tracking software releases, it outlines the evolution of the intellectual framework through a series of research volumes, frameworks, software, and community initiatives.

The roadmap is directional rather than prescriptive and will evolve as the field matures.

---

# Vision

Engineer trust into Industrial AI by combining engineering knowledge, grounded reasoning, decision governance, industrial agents, and operational systems into a coherent engineering discipline.

---

# Research Themes

The long-term research program is organized around seven enduring themes.

1. Engineering Knowledge
2. Grounded Reasoning
3. Trustworthy Decisions
4. Industrial Agents
5. Industrial AI Platforms
6. Operational Deployment
7. Industrial AI Products

These themes are expected to remain stable even as technologies evolve.

# Research Volumes

## Volume I

### The Governed Decision Layer

**Status**

In Progress

**Objective**

Introduce the architectural layer responsible for transforming AI reasoning into trustworthy operational decisions.

Deliverables

- 17-part article series
- Foundational vocabulary
- ASSET-grade framework
- Book manuscript (initial)
- Reference diagrams

---

## Volume II

### Grounded Reasoning

**Status**

Planned

**Objective**

Explore how engineering knowledge, optimization, causal reasoning, physics-informed AI, and domain constraints improve decision quality.

Potential topics

- Engineering memory
- Causal reasoning
- Physics-informed AI
- Optimization
- Simulation
- Time-series reasoning

---

## Volume III

### Engineering Knowledge

**Status**

Planned

**Objective**

Describe how engineering knowledge should be represented, managed, validated, and reused within Industrial AI systems.

Potential topics

- Knowledge graphs
- Standards
- Asset models
- Procedures
- Institutional knowledge
- Digital twins

---

## Volume IV

### Industrial Agents

**Status**

Exploratory

**Objective**

Define trustworthy industrial agent architectures that collaborate with engineers while operating within governed decision boundaries.

Potential topics

- Multi-agent systems
- Tool use
- Human-agent collaboration
- Planning
- Runtime governance
- Earned autonomy

---

## Volume V

### Industrial AI Platforms

**Status**

Vision

**Objective**

Describe the reference architecture for enterprise-scale Industrial AI platforms.

Potential topics

- Control planes
- Data products
- AI factories
- Operational knowledge hubs
- AI platform architecture

# Success Indicators

This initiative will be considered successful when:

- Concepts are cited by researchers.
- Frameworks are adopted by practitioners.
- Vocabulary becomes part of Industrial AI discussions.
- Universities reference the material.
- Industrial organizations use the reference architectures.
- Open-source contributors extend the ecosystem.

---

# Guiding Principle

Technology will continue to evolve.

The objective of this initiative is not to follow every technology trend, but to establish enduring engineering principles for Trustworthy Industrial AI.
