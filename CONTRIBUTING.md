# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in the Engineering Trustworthy Industrial Decisions in the Age of AI research initiative.

This project is intended to grow as an open body of knowledge for the Industrial AI community.

Contributions are welcome.

---

# Philosophy

This is primarily a research initiative—not a software project.

We value:

- engineering rigor
- clarity of thought
- practical relevance
- intellectual honesty
- evidence-based reasoning
- respectful technical discussion

---

# Types of Contributions

Contributions may include:

- corrections
- clarifications
- references to relevant standards
- improvements to diagrams
- architectural reviews
- industrial case studies
- literature references
- implementation examples
- software
- documentation improvements

---

# Before Contributing

Please first determine whether your proposal:

- strengthens Engineering Trustworthy Industrial Decisions in the Age of AI
- aligns with the research vision
- improves clarity
- adds evidence
- avoids unnecessary complexity

---

# Research Principles

Contributions should be:

Grounded.

Explainable.

Evidence-based.

Vendor neutral.

Industry applicable.

---

# Terminology

Please use canonical terminology whenever possible.

Definitions are maintained in:

TERMS.md

New terminology should not be introduced without discussion.

---

# Issues

Please use GitHub Issues for:

- corrections
- factual errors
- improvement suggestions
- requests for clarification
- references to new research

---

# Discussions

GitHub Discussions are preferred for:

- architectural ideas
- emerging concepts
- industry experiences
- future research directions

---

# Pull Requests

Pull Requests are welcome.

Please:

- explain the motivation
- keep changes focused
- reference supporting material where appropriate
- update documentation if terminology changes

---

# Large Contributions

Before investing significant effort, please open a Discussion describing:

- the problem
- proposed approach
- expected value

This helps ensure alignment with the long-term research roadmap.

---

# Code Contributions

Software repositories may use Apache License 2.0 or another repository-specific license.

# Code of Conduct

Be respectful.

Challenge ideas—not people.

Evidence is preferred over opinion.

Professional disagreement is encouraged.

Personal attacks are not.

---

# Thank You

Every meaningful contribution—whether a typo correction, architectural review, industrial experience or new research insight—helps advance the engineering discipline of Trustworthy Industrial AI.
